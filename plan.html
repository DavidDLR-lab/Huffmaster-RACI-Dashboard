Huffmaster — 60-Day Project Plan (example)

Every one of the 112 actions from the Workflow_Actions tab, scheduled across 60 working days. Stages overlap where the real work does; each gate is a milestone that closes its stage.
Illustrative schedule. Durations are derived from each action's checklist depth (tasks + subtasks), then fitted to its stage window — they are not client-confirmed dates. Day 1 = Mon 20 Jul 2026; weekends excluded.
